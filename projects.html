School Projects & Achievements

During my academic journey, I have participated in several research projects that helped me develop my skills in teamwork, problem-solving, and practical application. Some of these projects include:

Material Development from Banana Pseudostem Fiberboard

A research project focused on creating sustainable fiberboard materials using banana pseudostem fibers combined with epoxy resin. The study evaluated the material’s durability, environmental resistance, cost-efficiency, and mechanical strength, providing insights into practical and eco-friendly construction materials.

Student Entry-Exit Monitoring Application

A research project that proposed the development of a monitoring system for schools. This system allows teachers to record student entry and exit, while automatically sending notifications to parents. The project helped improve my skills in planning, research, and problem-solving.

These projects have given me valuable experience in research, collaboration, and applying engineering concepts to real-world problems, strengthening both my academic and practical skills.
