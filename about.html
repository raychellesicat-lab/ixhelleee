About Me

My name is Raychelle Marie Sicat, but most people call me Chelle or Shel. I am 19 years old and currently a first-year student taking Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering at Bataan Peninsula State University. I live in Mariveles, Bataan, but I study in Balanga City, which means I commute regularly for my classes. Although commuting can sometimes be tiring, it also motivates me to stay disciplined and focused on my studies.

I graduated from Lamao National High School, where I took the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) strand during senior high school. My experience in STEM helped me develop my interest in problem-solving, research, and analytical thinking. During that time, I also participated in several academic projects and research activities that strengthened my teamwork, leadership, and critical thinking skills.

As a person, I would describe myself as soft-hearted and emotional, but also determined when it comes to achieving my goals. I tend to feel things deeply, which helps me become more understanding and empathetic toward others. At the same time, I try my best to stay strong and motivated, especially when facing challenges in school or in life.

Outside of academics, I enjoy activities that help me relax and express myself. I like singing, playing chess, watching emotional movies, and listening to music, especially songs by The Weeknd. I also enjoy photographing sunsets and peaceful scenery, because these moments help me appreciate the simple things in life.
